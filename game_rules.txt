=== Rules ===

Board has 40 spaces
Each round the 10 traps and 4 heals appear at a random spaces #not all added into events
Players start with 3 hearts


=== Player Turn ===
Roll a dice to move or draw an item card or use as many item cards as you want
If you land on a heal gain a heart
If you land on a trap draw a trap card and do what it says
    things like lose a heart, go back etc
If you draw an item card you lose the chane to move on that turn, but next turn instead of rolling you can play a card
    things like swap places with a player, roll 2 dice this turn etc
Some item cards will prevent a trap or cause a trap
If you lose all your hearts discard all cards and go back to beginning with 2 hearts
    If it happens again, restart with 1 heart and no cards

At the moment only allows for landing on a trap

== Winning ==
Player who reaches the end wiht the most hearts
